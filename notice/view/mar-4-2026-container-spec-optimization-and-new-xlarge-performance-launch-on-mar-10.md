# (Mar 4, 2026) Container Spec Optimization & New XLarge Performance Launch on Mar 10

Hello, this is the Arkain Customer Experience team.

We are writing to inform you that **container spec optimizations** and the **new XLarge performance** tier will be implemented starting **March 10, 2026**.
