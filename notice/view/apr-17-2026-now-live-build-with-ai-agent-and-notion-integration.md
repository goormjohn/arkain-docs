#### What You Can Do with Arkain

⚡ **Instant Dev Environment**

Spin up a container-based development environment in seconds. Every project starts in its own isolated container, so you can build, experiment, and break things freely.

🤖 **AI Agent in Your Side Chat**

Tell Arkain what you want to build or fix — in plain language. The AI Agent breaks it down into steps and handles the rest: writing code, editing files, running commands, all inside your container.

New to Arkain? You get 3 free runs on Arkain's AI features. No strings attached.
