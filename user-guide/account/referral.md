There are two ways to access your referral dashboard:

1. Gift Box Icon (Quick Access): Click the Gift Box icon (<i class="fa-gift">:gift:</i>) located at the bottom-left corner of the Dashboard page.

### How to Share Your Link

The Referral page provides easy-to-use tools for sharing your unique invite information.

1. Generate Your Code: Click the \[Refresh] button to generate your unique referral code for the first time or to reset it.
2. Sharing Options:

### How Rewards are Split

Rewards are issued in two stages to both the Referrer (You) and the Referred Friend.

#### Step 1. Friend Signs Up

When a friend joins Arkain using your link or enters your code manually:

* Friend: +300 credits (Issued instantly)
* You: +300 credits (Issued instantly)

#### Step 2. First Credit Purchase ($10 or more)

When your friend makes their first credit purchase of $10 or more:

* Friend: +600 credits
* You: +600 credits
* Note: These rewards are issued after a 7-day confirmation period. Please note that the first transaction must be at least $10 to qualify for the reward.

***

### Important Notes

* Retroactive Application: Referral codes must be entered during signup. They cannot be added after the account is created.
* Credit Validity: Referral credits are valid for 60 days. Unused credits will expire automatically.
* Invite Limit: Each account is limited to 10 successful referrals.
* Reward Eligibility: The Purchase Reward is only granted if the first transaction is $10 or more. Cumulative purchases do not qualify.
* Refund Policy: If a purchase is refunded within the 7-day period, the Purchase Reward will be canceled.
